Back to School Checklist for Moms

Summer is winding down, and it's time to get our little ones ready for a new school year! Here’s my tried-and-true checklist to make the transition smooth and stress-free.

  • School supplies: pencils, notebooks, crayons
  • New backpack and lunchbox
  • Healthy snack options
  • Updated schedule and calendar
  • First-day outfit ready
  • Label all personal items
  • Review homework station setup

With a little planning and organization, you can make the back-to-school season exciting for both you and your child. Here’s to a happy and successful year ahead!
