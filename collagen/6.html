Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to take collagen?

+

Collagen can be taken any time of day. Many people prefer morning with coffee or post-workout for recovery. Consistency is more important than timing.

How long before I see results?

+

Most people notice improvements in skin hydration within 4-6 weeks, joint comfort in 8-12 weeks, and hair/nail strength in 12-16 weeks of consistent use.

Are there any side effects?

+

Collagen is generally well-tolerated. Some people may experience mild digestive upset initially. Start with a smaller dose and gradually increase.
